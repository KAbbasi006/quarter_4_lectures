
We’ll combine:
**🎭 Role Prompting + 🧩 Contextual Prompting + 🔗 Chain-of-Thought + 📋 Structured Output**

---

## 🧠 **Example Combined Prompt**

> **Prompt:**
> You are a **professional graphic designer** working at *Komal’s DesignVerse*, a creative studio that specializes in minimalist and modern brand identities.
>
> **Task:** Suggest **three unique logo ideas** for a new *organic skincare brand* called **"GlowLeaf"**.
>
> **Context:**
>
> * Target audience: women aged 20–35
> * Brand values: purity, sustainability, elegance
> * Preferred colors: green, beige, white
>
> **Instructions:**
>
> 1. Think **step-by-step** about the concept behind each idea.
> 2. For each logo, give:
>
>    * A short **name** for the design idea
>    * A **description** of the visual concept
>    * Recommended **color palette**
>    * Suitable **font style**
>
> **Output Format:**
> Present the answer in a clean bullet-point list with short paragraphs.

---

## 💡 **What This Prompt Does (Technique Breakdown)**

| Technique                   | Function                                             | Example in This Prompt                                  |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| 🎭 **Role Prompting**       | Gives the model a *persona* (expert designer)        | “You are a professional graphic designer…”              |
| 🧩 **Contextual Prompting** | Adds background info so model understands your brand | “Target audience… brand values… preferred colors…”      |
| 🔗 **Chain-of-Thought**     | Encourages step-by-step creative reasoning           | “Think step-by-step about the concept…”                 |
| 📋 **Structured Output**    | Controls how the answer is organized                 | “For each logo, give: name, description, colors, font…” |

---

## ✅ **Sample AI Output (What You’ll Get)**

**1. Nature’s Whisper**

* **Concept:** A delicate leaf forming a woman’s face outline — symbolizes natural beauty and purity.
* **Colors:** Olive green, soft beige.
* **Font:** Elegant serif (like Playfair Display).

**2. PureDrop**

* **Concept:** A water droplet merging with a leaf tip — shows purity and freshness.
* **Colors:** Mint green, off-white.
* **Font:** Minimal sans-serif (like Poppins Light).

**3. Glow Embrace**

* **Concept:** Circular emblem with sun rays and a leaf border — reflects warmth and self-care.
* **Colors:** Pastel green, sand beige, white.
* **Font:** Rounded sans-serif (like Quicksand Regular).

---

This is the **power of prompt engineering**:
By giving the AI the **right role, context, reasoning steps, and structure**, you get creative, relevant, and high-quality results — just like working with a real designer teammate 💫
